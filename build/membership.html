    Join Us

    WE ARE AN ‘OHANA

    When you become a member of the IIDA Hawaii Pacific Chapter, you are not only joining an international network of industry professionals, but you are also joining an established and fun design community here in our island home. Our strength comes from our members and we always look forward to meeting new faces! To learn more about each level of membership and what you get from joining this organization? Check out membership descriptions, benefits, appellations and associated costs at http://www.iida.org/content.cfm/membership.

    STUDENTS

    We are proud to partner with and support our local Universities and interior design students. After all, they truly are the future of our industry. Students are always welcome and encouraged to join our Chapter’s events and mingle with other industry professionals as they prepare for their future careers.     Membership Levels

    Want to learn more about each level of membership and what you get from joining this organization? Check out membership descriptions, benefits, appellations and associated costs at http://www.iida.org/content.cfm/membership

    Professional

    Sign Up

    Professional Members are defined as actively engaged in the profession of interior design or design education.

    ELIGIBILITY

    Professional members are defined as actively engaged in the profession of interior design or design eduction

    INTERIOR DESIGNER

    Proof of certification date (or test results) by NCIDQ. No other exams are accepted

    ARCHITECT

    Proof of either state architectural registration or documentation of successful completion of NCARB exam, and documentation (resume or current job description, with dates, acceptable) of minimum of six years practice experience in interior design above and beyond those areas tested by the NCARB exam.

    Associate

    Sign Up

    Associate Members are defined as actively engaged in the profession, but have not taken the accredited exam.

    ELIGIBILITY

    Associate Members are defined as actively engaged in the profession, but have not taken the accrediting exam.

    Have NOT taken the NCIDQ
    Meet the educational requirements for NCIDQ
    Submit a current resume
    Submit transcripts/diploma

    Educator

    Sign Up

    If you are a full-time educator or a department chair, we invite you to join us as a Professional or an Associate Member at reduced membership rates. Membership entitles you to receive publications, attend special events and access research and materials that benefit your students.

    ELIGIBILITY
    EDUCATOR PROFESSIONAL

    Full time educators and department chairs that meet the Professional Member requirements.

    EDUCATOR ASSOCIATE

    Full time educators and department chairs that meet the Associate Member requirements.

    Student

    Sign Up

    IIDA Student Members are the future of design. With your membership you are able to access the best industry events, the latest design trends, cutting edge resources and unparalleled networking.

    ELIGIBILITY

    Please provide ONE of the following with your application. This must be generated by your school and include your name. Applications received without proof of enrollment cannot be processed.

    Photocopy of current school ID or copy of transcript opportunities wherever you are located.

    Copy of current class schedule
    Professor’s signature (below)

    Business Card

    Industry

    Sign Up

    If you work for a manufacturer of interior design products or you are a rep for a manufacturer, IIDA Industry Membership offers tremendous networking opportunities to promote your company and services to IIDA Members. Companies joining as an Industry Member are allowed 3 to 30 Industry Reps as part of their membership, giving you further penetration into different Chapters around the world. Individual Industry Memberships are also available.

    BENEFITS

    As an Industry Member, you can take advantage of association programs that include:

    • Industry Roundtable
    • Chapter Leadership Council
    • Design Competitions
    • Access to the IIDA Email and Mail Lists
    • Event/Program Sponsorship Opportunities
    • Develop & Present CEU Courses
    • Special Member advertising rates in Perspective and Design Matters
    • Special Member rate for IIDA Events and Competitions
    • Special Member rental rate for IIDA’s Conference & Learning Center
    • Use of the IIDA Industry Logo
    • Company recognition in IIDA’s Perspective magazine
    • Reduced or complimentary job posting rates on IIDA’s website
    • Affiliation with a local IIDA Chapter
    • Invitations to International and Chapter events
    • Participation in IIDA ForumsRB exam.

    International

    Sign Up

    International Dual Membership and International At-Large Membership reflects the Association’s international focus. These Membership levels allow you to take advantage of Membership benefits and networking opportunities wherever you are located.

    ELIGIBILITY
    INTERNATIONAL AT-LARGE

    If you an active Interior Designer located outside of an area covered by an IIDA chapter, you can apply as an International At-Large Member. Intenational At-Large Members can join at the Professional, Associate, or Student level. With your application, you must submit:

    • Interior Design certificate, degree or diploma
    • Resume showing you have completed the relevant work experience
    • Access the At-Large application
    INTERNATIONAL DUAL

    If you are already an active Member with one of the Associations listed below, and are located outside the US, you can qualify as an International Dual Member. IIDA offers you a reduced rate to become a Member at a Professional, Associate, or Retired level. When you have IIDA as a secondary Membership you automatically have a network of designers and a roster of events that can help you get more out of your career:

    • BSID, Bermuda
    • IDC, Canada
    • BIID, UK
