    Events

    We like to have fun!

    The IIDA Hawaii Pacific Chapter offers many opportunities for members to meet and network with one another throughout the year. It is through these events that we foster collaboration and make the valuable connections necessary to move forward and succeed together in our industry. We invite you to join us at our next event! Please refer to our calendar of events to see what’s coming up. And remember -- events are open to everyone, members as well as non-members.
