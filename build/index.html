    Welcome

    E komo mai

    Aloha and welcome to the official website for the International Interior Design Association (IIDA) Hawaii Chapter. We are excited to embark on this new journey as a chapter with you! We believe the future of design is in our hands and through this organization, we are surrounded by the tools and people necessary to learn, grow and succeed in our profession. Together, we are leading the way for the future of our industry. Together, we bridge our passion, ourrelationshipsand our work for the greater community.

    Our Mission

    IIDA’s mission stands for the future of design. With respect for past accomplishments of Interior Design leaders, IIDA strives to create a strong niche for the most talented and visionary Interior Design professionals, to elevate the profession to the level it warrants, and to lead the way for the next generation of Interior Design innovators. The Association provides a forum to demonstrate design professionals’ impact on the health, safety, well-being and virtual soul of the public, balancing passion for good design and strategy for best business practices.
