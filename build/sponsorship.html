    Sponsorship

    IIDA Sponsorship

    Much of what our chapter does and provides is not possible without the help and support of our sponsors. Industry partners become a part of our team. We work with one another to craft programs and activities that enhance the chapter and provide even more value for our members. It is this synergy and collaboration that strengthens our professional capabilities and contributes to the growth and ultimate success of our chapter.


    We have been very fortunate to have amazing partners throughout the years and we continue to seek support and guidance as we evolve.

    Why be a partner?

    Expect MORE

    IIDA advances the profession of interior design through the progressive cultivation of innovative leadership and by constantly advocating for exceptional design.

    Practice MORE

    IIDA tracks and supports legislation that raises the bar for the profession and protects your right to practice

    Do MORE

    IIDA forums offer a venue for discussion with professionals and dissemination of information on trends, problem-solving strategies and perspectives on current or developing areas of design practice within specific markets

    Learn MORE

    Design is an emotional aesthetic and intellectual pursuit. Engage your passion. Satisfy your curiosity. Stay current with the latest discourse in design with news and info updates from IIDA and your chapter

    Know MORE

    Education is a life-long pursuit, and does not end when you graduate. IIDA supports your professional development with continuing education programs, providing knowledge and confidence to prosper in today’s evolving marketplace

    Say MORE

    We support, encourage and convene avid, active discourse about the issues and forces that are shaping, changing and pushing the profession

    Grow MORE

    IIDA is a catalyst for your professional development

    Connect with us:

    Want to know more? Curious about what we’re up to and what we’re all about? Let’s chat.

    Become a member of the ohana

    As IIDA members, we are an ‘ohana – a family. Creating connections and forging partnerships is what we’re all about. We welcome you to join our growing community and look forward to the synergies we can create together. Are you ready?
